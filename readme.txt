Aggregate and embed your social media posts on your site (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and many more) as a beautiful social media stream.

== Description ==
SocialMediaStream is a service that allows you to enter in the name of your social media accounts and SocialMediaStream.nl will automatically extract the posts from these social media networks and embed them into any of your pages with an easy shortcode. SocialMediaStream.nl will automatically update the streams whenever you create posts for your social media accounts.

To use this plugin you must create an account on [https://www.socialmediastream.nl](https://www.socialmediastream.nl). Then you must create a stream and add social media sources to it.

= Features =

Visit [https://www.socialmediastream.nl](https://www.socialmediastream.nl) for more information

== Installation ==
Download the plugin zip file, unzip it, and upload it to your wordpress site under the /wp-content/plugins directory.

Sign up via [https://www.socialmediastream.nl](https://www.socialmediastream.nl) and create a free trial account.

Once you've done that you can use the shortcode: `[socialmediastream stream="YOUR_STREAM_ID"]` in any post or page

To get your stream shortcode:

- Sign into your account on https://www.socialmediastream.nl
- Copy the shortcode from the left side menu.

Alternately, you can add the feed to your template directly if you like using PHP. Here's an example: `<?php socialmediastream('stream=YOUR_STREAM_ID'); ?>`
